Orbit K2-141B

The Earth-size exoplanet, named K2-141b, is a hot lava world on one side, but extremely cold on the other. It has a magma ocean, a rock vapor atmosphere, and supersonic winds.
$400,000 - $1.2m

Ice Skating on the Rings Of Saturn

Why not gift your someone special with an unforgettable experience. The rings of Saturn are the most extensive ring system of any planet in the Solar System so you can be sure it will be a memorable one.
$200,000 - $600,000

Meet the Martians!

Have you ever wished you could get closer and really get to know the people of destinations you visit? Well, we’ve come up with a way where you can explore different types of food, culture and natural sights during your vacation, as well as live with locals in various parts of Mars. From participating in traditional farming activities to being taught how to create your own piece of art, you will have the chance to get up close with the local inhabitants and feel their genuine warmth and welcome. Each village has its own speciality and craftsmanship, so do pick the one that you feel more related to.
